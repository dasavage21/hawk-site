Safety Standards & CSAE Policy

BlazeMates has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We:

  • Prohibit any sexual content involving minors and promptly remove and report violations.
  • Provide in-app tools to block & report users, and respond quickly to abuse reports.
  • Cooperate with law enforcement where appropriate.
  • Restrict the app to adults; age gate and enforcement included.
