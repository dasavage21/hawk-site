Child Safety Standards

BlazeMates prohibits any content that exploits or endangers children. We actively detect, report, and remove Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) content.

Our commitments

  • No tolerance for CSAE content or behavior; accounts are immediately terminated.
  • Photo and chat reporting tools available in-app; reports are reviewed 24/7.
  • We preserve evidence and report suspected CSAE to the appropriate authorities and NCMEC where applicable.
  • Automatic scanning of uploads against known CSAE hashes; suspicious activity is blocked and flagged.
  • Under-age use is prohibited; violators are removed and devices may be blocked.
