Child Safety Standards

BlazeMates is for adults 21+. We prohibit child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) content and behavior.

Our Standards

  • Strict age-gating and reporting tools in-app.
  • Zero tolerance for grooming, sexualization of minors, or CSAE content.
  • Swift removal and account termination upon verified violation.
  • Cooperation with law enforcement as required by law.

How to Report

Use in-app reporting or email safety@yourdomain.com.
